# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in the `speccheck` project should remain respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the software and documentation.

Examples of expected behavior:

- using welcoming and professional language
- giving and receiving technical feedback in good faith
- being explicit about assumptions, tradeoffs, and evidence

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or conduct
- deliberate disruption of project discussions or reviews

## Reporting

Report concerns to the maintainers through the repository issue tracker or the maintainer email listed in the package metadata.
